Chicken Alfredo

Description

The creamy cheesy flavor of this simple, easy to make, homemade alfredo sause is so delicious you will want to store some in the freezer for a quick meal another day!

Ingredients

  • 1 LB fettuccine
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese
  • 3 cloves fresh garlic
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 2 Lbs fresh chicken breast
  • 1 tbsp ground paprika
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • (Optional) 1 head fresh broccoli

Steps

    Chicken

  1. Cut the chicken into two inch strips and place in a small bowl.
  2. Mix in the paprika, salt and pepper into the chicken.
  3. Cover and put in the fridge to marinate for at least 20 minutes.
  4. Cook the chicken a skillet over medium heat for about 15 minutes or until the internal temperature is 165℉

    5. Alfredo Sauce (About 20 minutes)

  5. Over meduim heat, place the garlic and butter in a small sauce pan.
  6. let the garlic and butter simmer until the garlic is lightly browned. (Be careful not to over cook the garlic.)
  7. Mix in the heavy cream and parmesan cheese.
  8. Stir occasionally, until it starts to bubble consistently.
  9. Turn to warm heat and let it simmer until it thickens, about 5 minutes. stirring occasionally.

    10. Fettuccine

  10. Follow the directions on the package for your desired tenderness.
