Meatballs

Description

This is my favorite meatball recipe. The flavor is amazing and these delicious meatballs will melt in your mouth!

You will have amazing homemade meatballs, made from fresh ingredients, in about an hour that the whole family will start begging you to make!

Ingredients

  • 2 Lbs Ground Beef
  • 1 medium onion
  • 3/4 cups parmesian cheese
  • 1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 1/2 cups Italian style bread crumbs
  • 2 tsp (3 cloves) fresh garlic
  • 2 Tbsp parsley
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper

Steps

  1. In a small dish mix the spices. (salt, pepper and parsley)
  2. In a large bowl mix the ground beef, garlic, onion, cheese, egg and bread crumbs.
  3. Mix in the spices.
  4. Knead the mixture into a giant ball of meat.
  5. Pre heat the oven at 325℉.
  6. Prepare two baking sheets with parchment paper.
  7. Separate the mixture into 3/4 inch meatballs and place them on the prepared baking sheets.
  8. Bake for 25 minutes. turning the meatballs at least twice to prevent them from burning.
  9. The meatballs are done when they are lightly browned and have an internal temperature of 165℉
