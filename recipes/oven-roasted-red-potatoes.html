Oven Roasted Red Skin Potatoes

Description

These crispy, delicious, easy to make potatoes are so good you can eat them with anything!

Ingredients

  • Nonstick cooking spray
  • 1 Lb small red skin potatoes
  • 1 1/2 tsp olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425℉. Line a 15 x 10 x 1 inch baking pan with foil and lightly coat with cooking spray.
  2. Quarter or halve the potatoes. (You want about 1 inch pieces)
  3. In a large bowl, toss potatoes with olive oil, salt and pepper.
  4. Arrange the potatoes in a single layer on the prepared baking pan.
  5. Roast for 25 to 30 minutes or until outsides of the potatoes are lightly brown and cripsy and centers are tender. Stir at least once.
